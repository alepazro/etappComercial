Easily Install and Set Up

EasiTrack's Telemetry feature will allow you to control up to four (4) gadgets or functions in each one of your vehicles. Installation requires a relay connected to the circuit of each gadget. Professional installer recommended.

Setting up each output in the system is really simple. You can give each one a friendly name. You can also name each state of the relay, ON or OFF.

Connect up to 4 digital inputs

Our GPS Tracker offers you up to four (4) digital INPUTS, to learn about the status of 4 gadgets in your vehicle:


  • Is any door open while driving?
  • Are the seat belts buckled?
  • Is the alarm set?

You can virtually connect any gadget(**) of your vehicle to the digital inputs of the EasiTrack GPS Tracker, and learn about its state.

(**) Professional installers recommended. EasiTrack is not responsible for an improper installation and its effects.

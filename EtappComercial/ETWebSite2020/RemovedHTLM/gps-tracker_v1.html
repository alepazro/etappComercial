GPS Tracker

Generates and transmits the tracking information up to minute by minute to the GPS Tracking system.

GPS Antenna

Receives the satellite location information every second and feeds the GPS Tracker.

GSM Antenna

Enables the communication of the tracking data from the GPS Tracker to the data center, using the digital cellular network.

Electrical Harness

Connects the GPS Tracker to the electrical circuit of the vehicle to obtain power and to permit the GPS Tracker to detect different states of the vehicle upon which tracking data is generated.

More features:

  • Intelligent

    How Come?

    • EasiTrack's devices can be configured to react to multiple extenal events like time, movement, ignition changes, heading changes, speeding, idling, etc.
    • The EasiTrack device has a built-in three-axis digital accelerometer to detect motion.
    • The device also has an internal battery with overload protection and battery sensing.

    What's in it for you?

    • You will get all types of real time events:

      • Turn ON, reported the instant the ignition is turned ON.
      • Turn OFF, reported the instant the ignition is turned OFF.
      • Driving, reported minute by minute.
      • Idle, reported after 10 minutes of idling.
      • Stop, reported every 60 minutes when the Ignition is OFF
      • Speeding Alerts: You set the threshold and the system reports everytime the vehicle goes above it.
      • Idle Alerts: Same as Speeding Alerts
      • Geofences Alerts: You create as many geofences as you need, and the system will notify you everytime a geofence is crossed.
      • Inputs Alerts: On an advanced installation, you can be alerted of doors opening/closing, PTOs activation/deactivation, and more...
      • Driving behavior reporting based on Rapid Acceleration and Harsh Braking
      • The internal battery will allow the unit to continue reporting for a few hours even after the vehicle's battery has been disconected.
    • If you want an advanced implementation, you can also connect the 2 inputs to things like doors or PTOs so that you are notified everytime a door is open or closed, or a PTO is activated or inactivated.

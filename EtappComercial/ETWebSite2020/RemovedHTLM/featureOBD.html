Easy Installation with the OBD2 Power Connector

Install your GPS Tracker in minutes with the OBD Add-On

The OBD2 Power Connector makes installation of the EasiTrack GPS tracking device easy. There are no wires to splice. Just connect the cable to the OBD2 port of your vehicle and the other end to the EasiTrack GPS tracking device.

All passenger vehicles manufactured in 1997 and later have an OBD2 port.

Install in 10 minutes

Installation takes just a few steps

To install, locate the vehicle’s OBD2 Port. Most of the time it can be found in the foot well of the vehicle on the drivers side.


Then plug the OBD Power Connector to the OBD2 Port and to the device.


Finally locate a covert spot for the GPS tracking device. Under or within the dash is usually a great spot. Affix the device with zip ties and or adhesive tape. Ensure that the unit is secure and cannot move freely.

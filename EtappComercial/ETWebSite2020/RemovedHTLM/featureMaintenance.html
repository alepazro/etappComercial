Preventive Maintenance Module

Extend the life of your vehicles and keeps your fleet productive

Control unlimited maintenance tasks

With EasiTrack's Maintenance Module, you can create unlimited preventive maintenance tasks based on Miles, Engine Hours, Time. Some examples of tasks include:

  • Oil Change
  • Tire Rotation
  • Insurance Renewal
  • Break pads changing
  • License Driver Verification
  • ... and much more

Create a schedule for each vehicle

You can program all the tasks individually for each one of your fleet's vehicles. You will be notified via Emails and/or SMS when a task execution is approaching. Thanks to the unique technology of EasiTrack's hardware, the system can keep track of the miles, engine hours, and calendar, to make sure every single task is accounted for, on time.

Maintenance History

The Maintenance module provides the option to register every maintenance and every fueling event. All the history of maintenances per vehicle will be kept in the module's history. You will have a perfect record, in one site, backed up for whenever you need it.

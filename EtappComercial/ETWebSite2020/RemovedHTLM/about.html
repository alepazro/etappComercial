About EasiTrack

Since 2004 and a team with over 15 years of experience in the GPS Tracking Industry, EasiTrack has made cutting edge navigation applications available to small, medium and large size companies all over North America. We serve a variety of industries including but not limited to: Automotive, Agricultural Services, Construction, Equipment Services, Food Services, Home & Commercial, Delivery & Equipment Services, Healthcare, Hospitality, Government & Municipalities, School Districts, Transportation, Towing and Trucking.
All software are designed and owned solely by us. Our engineers are perfectionists and are constantly developing and adding new features to the software. Our call center is available during all business days assisting customers on Level I and Level II.

Facts Performance and Reliability are our foundation stones. For this reason, we are constantly supervising the status of our servers and all network components. In addition, our databases are continuously fine-tuned in order to make sure that they are in optimal condition so as to provide immediate response to our users. With Field Data Technologies, every single vehicle and fleet is accounted for, providing protection of information of your business assets.

  • Field Data Technologies is providing location services to more than 750 companies in USA and some in Canada.
  • More than 5,500 vehicles are being tracked by our platform all-year-round.
  • Field Data Technologies utilizes Enfora Tracking devices, which have demonstrated throughout the years to be one of the most reliable and advanced manufacturers of trackers in the USA.
  • We have developed numerous GPS Tracking Platforms for different companies in America and Canada.
  • All of our data and processes are hosted in a Tier 1 state of the art security data center in Houston, Texas.
  • We have over 15 years of telematics experience and our ICT engineers are the best in industry.
  • Our integrated GPS Tracking platform has been shown to progress fleet operation, productivity and efficiency, which in turn means new business prospects.

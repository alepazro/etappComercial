Starter Disable

Remotely enable and disable the starter of your vehicle(s)

Control with ease the use of your automobiles from the comfort of your computer

Full control of your vehicle usage

From any internet connected computer and with one click of a mouse you can prevent your vehicles from starting. The car starter disable feature is included with our GPS system at no extra cost!


*Professional installation recommended. Requires a 12 volt automotive relay connected to EasiTrack's GPS Tracker and to the vehicle’s starter circuit. EasiTrack is not responsible for improper use, installation or adverse effects of this feature.

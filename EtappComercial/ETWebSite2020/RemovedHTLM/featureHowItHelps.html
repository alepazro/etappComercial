How It Helps

Take your business to the next level!

Running a small business presents continuous challenges when you need to track a fleet of vehicles. Assigning job calls efficiently is challenging without knowing each vehicle's location. These inefficiencies lead to high costs due to excessive mileage, high fuel costs, and overtime.

EasiTrack' easy-to-use web-based and mobile applications help you improve the way you manage your fleet by letting you know all the information about your vehicles and drivers behaviors. With all this information at your fingertips you can:

  • Lower fuel consumption
  • Track mileage
  • Create more efficient delivery routes
  • Track safety
  • Manage vehicle maintenance
  • Receive alerts or notifications when a vehicle enters or leaves a perimeter
  • Track vehicle speed
  • Improve customer service and estimated delivery time

EasiTrack goal is to enable small businesses with the technology and automation that larger corporations enjoy, at a fraction of the cost. With EasiTrack, we empower you with fleet intelligence that will increase your customers satisfaction, lower your operational costs, and bring more money to your bank account.

EASY OVER THE AIR SERVICING

Configuration parameters and firmware are upgradeable over-the air using IP or text messaging or autonomously pulled from any TFTP or FTP server; this allows feature updates to be deployed to a fleet in a very efficient manner.

MONITOR DRIVERS BEHAVIOR

Ability to detect the most important bad-behaviors of drivers, including: Accelerations, Sudden Decelerations, Harsh Brakes, and Harsh Turns. Every time the device detects any of these behaviors, it triggers events that are sent to our system where the data is collected and reported to you immediately.

SUPER HIGH GAIN INTERNAL ANTENNA

The antennas of our GPS device are internal. This facilitates incredibly the installation process, while providing an outstanding sensitivity that gives very accurate location information.
